Frequently Asked Questions

What if I don't have a lot of experience with programming or technology? Is this event for me?

A: Yes if you have fun while coding and you love to code then you are welcome to Pasckathon 2020.

If I am a member of a team that is attending, can we work together?

A: Yes! You can work with whoever you like.

A: Great! You will have an opportunity to hear different ideas "pitched" and you can decide which idea interests you and join a team.

A: No, as long as every member is individually registered in the system, the minimum is to have one member onsite.
