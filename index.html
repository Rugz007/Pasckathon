About


PASCKATHON 2020 is open for all event where participants can use any technology to build their hacks. Some of the suggested domains are: Machine Learning, Blockchain, Internet of Things, Artificial Intelligence, Big Data, Cyber Security, Cloud and DevOps.



First Round

For the first round, participants will be submitting a PowerPoint Presentation with their problem statement, ideas, solutions, tech stack and case diagrams.


Second Round

During the second round, qualified teams will gather at the given venue and implement their idea. This round will be for 18 hours. After the time is up, the teams will stop working on the hacks and present it in front of the judges. The judges will give scores based on the judging criteria. After evaluation of all projects, the winner and runner up will be de up will be declared.

FAQs



What if I don't have a lot of experience with programming or technology? Is this event for me?

A: Yes if you have fun while coding and you love to code then you are welcome to Pasckathon 2020.

If I am a member of a team that is attending, can we work together?

A: Yes! You can work with whoever you like.

What if i don't have a team and am coming as an individual?

A: Great! You will have an opportunity to hear different ideas "pitched" and you can decide which idea interests you and join a team.

Do all team members need to be present in person?

A: No, as long as every member is individually registered in the system, the minimum is to have one member onsite.

Do you provide accomodation, travel reimbursement and food?

A: No, it is on you.However we provide food.

What is the venue of the hackathon?

A: Pune Institute of Computer Technology
